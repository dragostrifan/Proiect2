Let's Go Now

If you are looking for adventure, here you can find the best hiking trails.


1.Short Inca Trail

1-2 Days

DAY 1 : OLLANTAYTAMBO - KM 104- AGUAS CALIENTES After breakfast, we will go to the Ollantaytambo train station to take the train to KM104. We will visit an Inca site before hiking for 4 hours to lunch at Wiñay Wayna. After lunch, we will continue for 2 more hours to the Sun Gate where you will have your first view of Machu Picchu. After spending some time there, you will take the shuttle to Aguas Calientes where you will have dinner and sleep in a comfy hotel.
DAY 2 : MACHU PICCHU - CUSCO Today is the highlight of your trek! You will have a 2 hour guided tour of the citadel with your guide before having time to explore on your own or climb Huayna Picchu Mountain. After your free time, you will take the shuttle back to Aguas Calientes before catching the train back to Ollantaytambo and your van to Cusco.

2.Ausangate Macchu Picchu

6-7 Days

DAY 1 : CUSCO - PACHANTA - UPIS Your adventure awaits!
We will pick you up from your hotel early in the morning for a GORGEOUS three hour drive to our starting point in Pachanta. You'll meet our staff and hit the trail for 3 hours on an easy, steady incline until we get to the lunch spot for our first amazing meal of the trek. Three more hours of this steady pace will bring us to the first campsite in Upis. You have the option to take a dip in the hot springs in Upis if you bring a swimsuit!
DAY 2 : UPIS - APARA PASS - PUKA QOCHA Hope you're feeling refreshed! After breakfast, we'll start the 3 hour climb to our first mountain pass - the Arapa Pass (4850m/15908 ft). We'll spend a little time here to celebrate our accomplishment before rewarding our legs and lungs with a descent to our next campsite. We will reach camp at Puka Qocha where the chef will be waiting with Andean happy hour and dinner.
DAY 3 : PUKA QOCHA - YANA QOCHA Earning our bragging rights today! After breakfast, we'll start the 2 hour trek around the Ausangate glacier to the Puka Pass (16,370ft) where we'll find the most beautiful turquoise, crystal clear lake surrounded by llamas, alpacas, and the snow-capped peak of Ausangate - the second highest mountain in Peru. Just when you think it can't get more beautiful, we'll start to descend for 2 hours and find our first views of the famous rainbow mountains until we get to lunch. With a full stomach, we'll hike for another 1.5 hours to the 3rd and final mountain pass - Warmisaya (14,370ft). We can spend as much time as we want here before hiking the last 30 minutes to our campsite.* *Before dinner, if the weather is nice, you will have the option to do an additional 1.5 hour round trip hike to Rainbow Mountain for sunset.
DAY 4 : YANA QOCHA - RAINBOW MOUNTAIN - OLLANTAYTAMBO Ready for a shower yet? We'll start the day off early with a 30 minute hike to Rainbow Mountain. We'll have the site to ourselves for the morning and can spend as much time here as we want. When we are ready to go, we will take the off-the-beaten-path trail through the spectacular red valley for 2 hours and will be treated to stunning sights around every corner. After reaching our lunch spot in Vinicunka, we will take a van for 3.5 hours to Ollantaytambo where you'll have a hotel with a comfy bed and hot shower for the night.
DAY 5 : OLLANTAYTAMBO - INCA TRAIL - AGUAS CALIENTES A change of scenery, but the fun's not over! We'll have breakfast at the hotel before taking a scenic train ride to the start of the short Inca Trail - KM 104. We'll climb through the cloudy forest on a trail and a maze of stone steps and Inca ruins until we reach the Inca site of Wiñay Wayna (Forevery Young). This is a tough day, but we'll be with you for every step and it will feel amazing at the end. From Wiñay Wayna, we will have a bite to eat and walk on an easy path to the entrance of Machu Picchu - the Sun Gate. After celebrating this incredible accomplishment, we'll hike down into Machu Picchu city for a quick look around before heading to Aguas Calientes and another nice hotel for the evening.
DAY 6 : MACHU PICCHU - CUSCO The icing on the cake! Today we go back to Machu Picchu (yes, you'll get to visit Machu Picchu twice!) for a 2 hour guided tour of the mysterious citadel. You have the option to add a Huayna Picchu climb or Machu Picchu mountain climb (if you book tickets in advance!) if you want to do something extra within the city. When you have explored all the highlights, you'll take the bus back to Aguas Calientes for lunch or a quick snack before your train back to Ollantaytambo. Our van will be waiting there to take you back to your hotel in Cusco.

3.Lares Trek

3-4 Days

DAY 1 : HUARAN- CANCHACANCHA Here we go! We will drive from Cusco to the head of the trail at Calca for breakfast. The first leg of the trek takes 4 hours until we get to lunch. After lunch, a 2 hour climb takes us to the campsite at Cancha Cancha for Andean Happy Hour and dinner.
DAY 2 : CANCHA CANCHA- QUISWARANY - LARES HOT SPRINGS The top is just around the corner! After a delicious breakfast, we start the three-hour climb to Pachacute Mountain for stunning views. We will spend some time here before descending to the lunch spot near a beautiful lake. After lunch, we continue descending for 3 hours to the campsite at Lares Hot Springs. You will have the opportunity to enjoy any of the various pools in Lares before or after dinner.
DAY 3 : LARES HOT SPRINGS - ANKASMARKA - AGUAS CALIENTES Almost done! After breakfast, we will say goodbye to the horsemen, chef, and porters. We will take the van for 1.5 hours to the final leg of your trek through the Ankasmarka Inca site. After a short downhill hike, we will take you to the Ollantaytambo train station for our ride to the town of Aguas Calientes. We will have dinner all together before sleeping at a hotel.
DAY 4 : MACHU PICCHU - CUSCO Today is the day! Today is the day you have been waiting for! We will get up early to take the bus to the entrance of Machu Picchu. After a 2 hour guided tour, you will have some time to explore the site on your own. When you are finished, you will take the bus back to Aguas Calientes for your train ride back to Ollantaytambo where your van will take you back to Cusco.
